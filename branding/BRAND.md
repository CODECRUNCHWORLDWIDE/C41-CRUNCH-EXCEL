# C41 · Crunch Excel — Brand Guide

- **Accent:** Code Crunch blue `#2463EB` (with deep `#1d4ed8`).
- **Voice:** practical and precise. Always show the cell, then the formula, then *why* it works — and where Excel and Google Sheets differ. No mystery clicks; every button has a keyboard path and a reason.
- **Icon motif:** a grid of cells with a spilling dynamic-array arrow.
- **One-liner:** "Master the spreadsheet — from SUM to dynamic-array dashboards and macros."
