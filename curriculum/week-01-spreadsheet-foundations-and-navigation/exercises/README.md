# Week 1 — Exercises

Three exercises, meant to be done in order, each about 45–60 minutes. Together they turn the three lectures into muscle memory: entering and formatting real data, drilling navigation shortcuts under a timer, and standing up a proper multi-sheet workbook.

## How to work these

- Use the same workbook you set up in the [week README](../README.md) (`crunch-week1.xlsx` or its Google Sheets equivalent) — add a new sheet per exercise so nothing overwrites earlier work.
- Do them **in order**. Exercise 2 assumes the shortcuts from Lecture 2; Exercise 3 assumes you can already format cleanly from Exercise 1.
- Work in whichever app is your primary this week, but try at least one task in the *other* app too — the goal is to notice what's identical and what isn't, while it's fresh.
- No formulas yet. That's Week 2. Everything here is entry, navigation, and formatting only.
- When a task says "Expected," check your result against it before moving on — catching a mistake in Exercise 1 is a lot cheaper than catching it in the mini-project.
